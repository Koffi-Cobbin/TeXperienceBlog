Creating a Post

  • To create a post, signup and login to your acount.
  • On your profile page, click on (+New Post) button at the top right side of the page.
  • This will take you to the New Post page.
  • Now, type:
  • The title of your post
  • The category of your post.( eg. Fashion, Tech, Food, Sports etc.)
  • The content of the post.
  • Select a cover image for the post. (optional)

    • We'll do the rest!
